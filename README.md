# turtle-game
<body>
Python / Pygame project developed from May - June 2024. 

<h3> Instructions </h3>
<p>Run "Main.py" to run the game.

<h4>Controls:</h4>
<b>Spacebar</b> - enable heat values <br>
<b>Left click & hold</b> - charge lightning <br>
<b>Left click release</b> - lightning strike <br>
</p>

<h2>DESCRIPTION</h2>
<p>
<b>This game was developed around a unique idea:</b> The sex of a sea turle is determined by the temperature of the egg before hatching.<br>

The concept was translated into gameplay by giving the player lighting to heat up the eggs, a tide which constantly cools the eggs, and waves of lizards who eat the eggs.
After 30 days, a score is assigned based on the number of eggs that successfully hatched and the ratio of male to female eggs.
</p>
